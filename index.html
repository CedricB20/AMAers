Q. Paano mag log in

You must have your USN na binigay sayo (extremely important)


  • A. In the ENTER EMAIL FIELD: Type in 11-digit USN
  • Follow the format for password:
  • Password=USN-Birthday
  • U=11 digit USN
  • YYYY – birth year in 4-digit format
  • MM – birth month in 2-digit format
  • DD – birth date in 2-digit format
  • Example is
  • USERNAME: 20001048800
  • PASSWORD: 2000104880020040106
  • The student’s birthday in this example is January 6, 2004.
